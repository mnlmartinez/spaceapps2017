  • Benefits

    A smart city will change dinamically as the conditions change and provide citizens with better health, more efficient energy and less congested roads.

    • Transport

      Lanes in the city will be reversed dinamically depending on the conditions to reduce travel times.

    • Energy

      Not only will the power generation be outside the city but various Nexus batteries will be distributed to provide continous energy.

    • Health

      Not only will ambulances reach their destinations faster but the city will have less pollution due to the reduced travel time and energy distribution.
